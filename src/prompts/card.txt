## Rules for domain_description
- Describe what the database is about: its domain, scope, main entities,
  and what kinds of questions it can answer.
- Mention ONLY relationships that appear in "Declared relationships" above.
  Do NOT infer or invent any new relationship.
- If sample questions are provided, make sure the entities and terms those
  questions refer to are mentioned in the description.
- Do not copy question sentences verbatim; describe content in your own words.

## Rules for term_glossary
- Gloss ONLY entity or field names whose meaning is not obvious from the
  name itself (abbreviations, codes, domain jargon).
- Write a gloss ONLY when the surrounding schema context is sufficient to
  infer the meaning. If you cannot infer it from the input, OMIT the name.
  An empty glossary {{}} is a valid answer.
- Each gloss is at most 20 words. Use names exactly as they appear in the
  inventory.

## General rules
- Use only information present in the input. Do not use outside knowledge
  about similarly-named datasets.
- Do not output scores, confidence, or any field other than the two above.
